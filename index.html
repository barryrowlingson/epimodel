Epidemic Modelling Simulations

Exponential growth/decay models

These models simulate potentially unlimited growth characterised by a reproduction rate, ρ, parameter. The simulation can either be deterministic or modelled as a Poisson process.

SIS models

A finite population with initial infectious cases. Individuals may recover but can get reinfected. There are two parameters here so that infection and recovery can be independently controlled. Randomness can be introduced by a Binomial distribution.

SIR models

An SIR model where infectious cases recover and are removed from the process.
